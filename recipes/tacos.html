Tacos

Description

This is the recipe for homemade tacos. These tacos use ground beef. Feel free to add any additional infredients that suit your preferences!

This sophisticated recipe was passed down to me from my mother.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 package hard shell tacos
  • 1 head of lettuce
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 package of taco seasoning
  • 1 package of colby jack cheese
  • 3/4 cup of water

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef on stovetop using medium heat
  2. Strain excess fat from ground beef
  3. Add water and taco seasoning to ground beef and stir
  4. Allow sauce to simmer on medium heat until it thickens
  5. Add seasoned ground beef to taco shells
  6. Top tacos with cheese, lettuce, and tomatoes
  7. Enjoy!
