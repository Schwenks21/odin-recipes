Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

Here is a very straightforward and simple recipe for making mac and cheese at home.

Feel free to add any additional ingredients to this mac and cheese recipe. This recipe will only cover the basics

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of pasta shells or noodles of your choosing
  • 2 cups of cheese of your choosing
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 2 cups of 2% milk

Steps

  1. In a sauce pan, boil water
  2. Once water is boiling, dump noodles into water and stir
  3. Once noodles are cooked (approx 8 minutes), remove saucepan from heat
  4. Strain water from noodles
  5. Add cheese, milk, and butter to noodles and stir
  6. Mix well
  7. Let sit 5 minutes to cool
  8. Enjoy!
