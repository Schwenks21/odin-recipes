Chili Recipe

Here is my go-to chili recipe.

You can't go wrong with this!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 jalapeno (or habanero if you prefer spicy)
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 large can tomato sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon cumin powder

Steps

  1. Ground beef on stovetop
  2. Strain fat from beef once browned
  3. Dice green pepper, onion, and jalepeno
  4. Combine beef, peppers, onion, jalapeno, beans, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes into large sauce pan
  5. Add chili powder, cayenne pepper, and cumin powder to saucepan
  6. Cover and let simmer until sauce thickens
  7. Enjoy!
